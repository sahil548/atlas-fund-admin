# Legal & Structural Due Diligence

Legal DD analysis on: $ARGUMENTS (file path to legal docs, or deal description)

## Analysis Scope

### Entity Structure
- Holding structure diagram (who owns what, through which vehicles)
- Tax implications of the structure (pass-through, blocker, UBTI)
- Jurisdictional risks

### Key Agreements
- Purchase/subscription agreement: key terms, reps & warranties, indemnification
- Side letters: any preferential terms to specific LPs
- Management agreement: fees, carry, clawback provisions
- Operating agreement / partnership agreement: governance, voting, consent rights

### Regulatory
- Required licenses and permits (status)
- Pending or threatened litigation
- Environmental compliance (Phase I/II if RE)
- OFAC / sanctions / KYC / AML screening

### Covenant Analysis (if debt)
- Financial covenants: type, threshold, test frequency, current headroom
- Negative covenants: restricted payments, additional indebtedness, liens
- Change of control provisions
- Default triggers and cure periods

### Risk Matrix
For each risk identified, provide:
| Risk | Severity | Likelihood | Mitigation | Owner |

## Output
Structured report. Flag deal-breakers in bold red. End with a list of conditions that must be satisfied before closing.
