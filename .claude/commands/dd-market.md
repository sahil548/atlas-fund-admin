# Market & Commercial Due Diligence

Market DD analysis on: $ARGUMENTS (deal name, sector, or file path to materials)

## Analysis Scope

### Market Sizing
- TAM / SAM / SOM with sources
- Market growth rate and key drivers
- Where we are in the cycle (early/growth/mature/declining)

### Competitive Landscape
- Top 5 competitors with market share estimates
- Competitive moats: brand, scale, IP, switching costs, network effects
- Threat of new entrants and substitutes
- Pricing power assessment

### Customer Analysis
- Customer segmentation and concentration
- Retention/churn rates (or lease renewal rates for RE)
- Customer acquisition cost and lifetime value
- Net Promoter Score or satisfaction indicators

### Industry Trends
- Tailwinds (what's helping)
- Headwinds (what's hurting)
- Regulatory direction
- Technology disruption risks

### Comparable Transactions
Search for recent comparable deals and present:
| Date | Target | Buyer | Deal Size | Multiple | Notes |

Use web search to find real comparable transactions from the last 2 years.

### Real Estate Specific (if applicable)
- Submarket supply/demand dynamics
- Rent comp analysis
- Vacancy trends
- Cap rate trends for the asset type and geography

## Output
Structured report with data tables. Include sources for market data. End with: is this a good market to deploy capital into right now, and why/why not.
