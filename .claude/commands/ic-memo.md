# Generate IC Memo

Generate an Investment Committee memo for: $ARGUMENTS (deal name or file path)

## Memo Structure

### Executive Summary (1 paragraph)
- What the deal is, size, why we're looking at it, and recommendation

### Investment Highlights
- 3-5 bullet points on why this is attractive

### Key Risks & Mitigants
| Risk | Impact | Likelihood | Mitigant |

### Deal Terms
- Structure (equity/debt, direct/co-invest/LP)
- Entry valuation and basis
- Expected hold period
- Target returns (IRR, MOIC, yield)
- Key protections (governance, consent rights, information rights)

### Financial Summary
| Metric | Trailing | Current | Projected |
Include revenue, EBITDA, margins, leverage, coverage

### DD Findings Summary
- Financial DD: key takeaways
- Legal DD: key takeaways
- Market DD: key takeaways
- Outstanding items / conditions

### Allocation Recommendation
- Which fund entity(ies) should participate
- Allocation amount per entity
- Co-invest opportunity for LPs? (Y/N, terms)
- Capital call timing

### Recommendation
**APPROVE / APPROVE WITH CONDITIONS / DECLINE**

Conditions (if any):
1. ...
2. ...

### Appendix
- Sources and materials reviewed
- Comparable transactions table
- Sensitivity analysis

## Formatting
Write this as a professional IC memo suitable for distribution to voting members. Use formal tone. Include page breaks between sections (markdown horizontal rules). Target 3-5 pages equivalent.
