## Why this milestone exists

v2.0 shipped a broad intelligence platform but left two structural gaps in the day-to-day GP workflow:

1. **No edit/delete/revert across most entities.** Users could create records but had to contact an engineer to correct mistakes.
2. **Waterfall math was approximately correct for textbook 4-tier European waterfalls but wrong for real-world fund accounting** — specifically simplified `{pref → remainder-to-GP}` templates, phased capital calls, year-end crossings, and distributions after ROC events.

Kathryn's 71 commits closed both gaps.

## Known debt entering v3.0

1. **`waterfall-templates/[id]/calculate/route.ts`** still inlines pref-accrual logic. Refactor to import from `pref-accrual.ts`.
2. **Side effect on distribution creation** (`a80d4c1`): backend updates `DistributionEvent` totals after line items are created. Downstream reports that read event totals before line items exist may see inconsistent data.
3. **Branch name `feat/edit-delete-across-entities`** is misleading — tip 6 commits are all pref-return fixes. Rename before archiving.
4. **No integration/E2E coverage** for the calculate route. Unit tests cover helpers; route-level integration tests against a seeded DB are absent.
5. **Validation against one fund only** (PCF II). Equity funds, European vs American waterfalls, multi-LP funds with different entry dates not exercised.
6. **Excel-specific conventions** (30/360 inclusive, ROC first-of-next-month effective date) documented inline but not in a top-level `docs/waterfall-conventions.md`.
