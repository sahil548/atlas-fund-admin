<objective>
Expand the side letter system from a free-text "terms" field to structured, computable rule types. Build the side letter fee adjustment engine that computes standard fees then shows side letter adjustments as separate line items. Add MFN (Most Favored Nation) gap detection.

Purpose: Side letters are the mechanism LPs use to negotiate custom terms. Without structured rules, Atlas cannot automatically adjust fees per LP — the GP has to do this manually in spreadsheets. This is Phase 4's financial backbone.

Output: SideLetterRule model, side letter engine (applySideLetterRules, detectMFNGaps), expanded CRUD API, and rules management UI.
</objective>
