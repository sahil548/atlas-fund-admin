<objective>
Close FIN-04: validate that the waterfall engine produces correct results for a fund other than CG Private Credit Fund II. Surface any PCF-II-specific assumptions baked into the engine.

**This plan is user-gated.** Per CONTEXT.md locked decision, the user provides a real-fund Excel (non-PCF-family) with a meaningfully different waterfall shape — equity fund, different catch-up style, different pref mechanism. Plan execution waits on the Excel.

Per CONTEXT.md:
- Multi-distribution lifecycle depth: at least three distributions — early ROC event, mid-fund income distribution, late-fund carry event.
- Discrepancy triage rule: Excel wins for convention differences (30/360 vs actual/360, inclusive vs exclusive day count, ROC-effective-date). Engine wins for bugs (wrong segment walk, wrong base calculation). Each discrepancy gets a written triage call in VERIFICATION.

Purpose: The engine has been validated only against PCF II. Any PCF-II-specific assumption (e.g., precomputed pref amount, specific catch-up structure, specific day-count convention) could produce wrong results for a different fund with the exact same user-facing "80/20 after 8% pref" description. Second-fund validation is how we find those assumptions.

Output: Either (a) engine passes validation unchanged (confidence in generality), or (b) discovered assumptions are documented and fixed. Conventions that differ go into Phase 23's FIN-03 conventions doc.
</objective>
