**Go to:** http://localhost:3000/transactions

### Capital Calls tab [CRUD-09/10/12, UX-10]

**What to look at:**
1. Do you see a list of capital calls?
2. Click on a capital call to open it. Can you see the line items (one row per investor)?
3. **Edit a line item [CRUD-09]:** Try editing a line item amount inline — does it save?
4. **Set a funded date [CRUD-09]:** On a line item, try setting or changing the funded date.
5. **Fund All button [UX-10]:** On a draft capital call, look for a "Fund All" button. If you see it, click it — do all line items get marked as funded at once?
6. **$0 line item [CRUD-10]:** Try creating a capital call with a $0 amount for one investor — does it allow $0?

### Distributions tab [CRUD-11, WF-20/21/23]

**What to look at:**
1. Do you see the list of distributions?
2. **Create a distribution [WF-24]:** Click "+ Distribution" (or similar). Choose an entity and enter an amount. Can you preview what the waterfall allocation would look like? Do the per-investor amounts look correct? [WF-23]
3. Is there a debug panel showing the waterfall math when you run the preview? [WF-20]
4. Does a "Preview" button work — can you enter an amount and click Preview to see the split? [WF-21]
5. **Edit a distribution [CRUD-11]:** On an existing distribution, try editing it and saving.
6. **Revert to draft [CRUD-11]:** Try reverting a distribution back to draft status.
7. **Delete a distribution [CRUD-11]:** Delete a test distribution — does it disappear?

**What to look at:**
