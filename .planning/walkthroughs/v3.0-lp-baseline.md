## Pass 1: Multi-Vehicle LP — CalPERS (Michael Chen)

**Sign in as:** Michael Chen (`user-lp-calpers`)

**How to switch users:** In the local dev app, find the user dropdown in the top-right corner and select "Michael Chen". You should see the initials "MC" in the corner after switching, and the sidebar should change from the GP navigation (Deals, Assets, Entities...) to the LP navigation (My Overview, Capital Account, LP Portfolio, Notices & Activity, Documents, Settings, Profile). If the sidebar doesn't change, the user switch didn't take — note it as a Blocker observation.

**What this tests:** CalPERS has commitments across 5 fund vehicles and has both funded and pending capital calls, plus distributions. This is the most data-rich LP view in the demo. It stresses the portal's ability to aggregate data across multiple funds and show a meaningful picture for a large institutional investor (CalPERS committed roughly $165M total).

---

### LP Domain 1: Dashboard (My Overview)

**Go to:** http://localhost:3000/lp-dashboard

**What to look at:**
1. Do the stat cards at the top show numbers? (Total committed capital, total called, total distributed, and similar summary numbers)
2. Do the numbers look plausible for a large institutional investor? CalPERS committed roughly $165M total — does the "Total Committed" number look roughly in that range?
3. Is there a commitments table showing all 5 fund vehicles CalPERS is invested in?
4. Does anything look wrong, missing, or confusing — numbers that seem off, sections that are blank, anything you'd expect to see that isn't there?
