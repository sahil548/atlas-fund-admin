### Pass 2 — LP Domain 2: Capital Account

**Go to:** http://localhost:3000/lp-account

**Capture block:**

### LP-Obs 2 — Capital Account distribution math DOES NOT TIE OUT — calc-critical

**Page / Action:** /lp-account (viewed as Tom Wellington) — distribution totals vs breakdown
**What I saw:** The page shows $14M in distributions but the breakdown only accounts for $4M income + $4M principal = **$8M accounted for, $6M unexplained**. The distribution total doesn't reconcile with its own itemized breakdown. This is exactly the kind of calc integrity failure Observation 20 (GP walkthrough) predicted — asset-level numbers rolling up to LP-level numbers with errors compounding along the way. Possible root causes:

1. The distribution total is over-counting (double-counting, including draft/cancelled distributions, or summing at wrong grain)
2. The itemized breakdown is missing distribution categories — return of capital, carry distributions, tax distributions, preferred return, etc. — so the sum can't match
3. A v2.1 waterfall change introduced categories the LP-account display wasn't updated to render
4. There is a flow-through category (e.g., recallable ROC) that's summed into total but intentionally not displayed — in which case the copy needs to explain why the breakdown doesn't sum to total

**Regardless of root cause, an LP looking at this can't reconcile their own statement** — this is a genuine correctness blocker for LP-facing reporting.

**Severity:**
- [x] Blocker — LP statement does not reconcile; unacceptable to send to a real LP
- [ ] Annoying
- [ ] Nitpick

**Page / Action:** /lp-portfolio (viewed as Tom Wellington) — per-asset / per-vehicle value display
**What I saw:** "Under LP portfolio it should show what their investment in that asset is vs what it's worth."
